## Project Overview
This project focuses on developing a complete digital marketing strategy for AL-HOUT, a restaurant and café equipment supplier in Egypt. The main challenge is increasing sales and brand awareness in a highly competitive market. The strategy includes paid advertising campaigns, content marketing, visual branding improvements, and market analysis to position AL-HOUT as a price leader with fast nationwide delivery.

---

## Project Objectives
- Increase sales by 15% within one month  
- Increase Facebook reach & engagement by 20%  
- Improve brand identity and credibility  
- Expand online presence and customer acquisition  

---

## Project Scope
- Market & competitor analysis (SWOT, STP)  
- Target audience identification  
- Facebook Ads strategy (Messages & Leads)  
- Content marketing plan (Reels & posts)  
- Visual branding enhancement  

> **Final Deliverable:** A complete digital marketing strategy presentation including analysis, action plan, and performance KPIs.

---

### Week 1 — Research & Analysis

### Week 4 — Campaign Setup

### Week 5 — Monitoring & Evaluation
